NATO, Gender and the Military

Women Organising from Within

Buy Now

Drawing on newly declassified NATO documents, this book:

  • Provides the first comprehensive account of gender at NATO
  • Examines the role of NATO Women and gendermen
  • Assesses NATO's engagement with Women, Peace and Security
  • Identifies examples of best practice
